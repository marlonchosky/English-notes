   - Look: Intransitive Verb (Prepositional Verb)
   - at the room: Prepositional Complement and Phrase
     - at: head word of the PP
2. I protected the house from fire
   - protected: Ditransitive Prepositional Verb
   - the house: DO and NP
   - from fire: Prepositional Complement of the Verb, and PP
3. I left him with the bill
   - left: Ditransitive Prepositional Verb
   - him: DO and pronoun
   - with the bill: Prepositional Complement of the verb and PP
