1. The workers repaired the equipment inside the shaft.

   - If you intend to mean that the repairment happened inside the shaft:

     - repaired: Monotransitive

     - the equipment: DO and NP

     - inside the shaft: Adverbial Adjunct of Place

   - If you intend to mean that the equipment was inside the shaft during the repairment

     - repaired: Monotransitive
     - the equipment inside the shaft: DO and NP
     - equipment: head word of the NP
     - inside the shaft: Adjectival Post-Modifier

   - If you intend that the repairment happened, and during this the equipment was inside the shaft

     - repaired: Monotransitive
     - the equipment: DO and NP
     - inside the shaft: Depictive Secondary Predicate

2. The workers extracted the ore carefully last night

   - extracted: Monotransitive
   - the ore: DO and NP
   - carefully: Adverbial Adjunct of Manner
   - last night: Adverbial Adjunct of Time, and NP
     - last: Post-Determiner / Adjective (**Deictic Adjective**-it points to a time relative to now))
     - night: head word

   <u>==Note==</u>: Saying "the last night" instead of "last night" changes the expression from a Deictic expression to a Relative expression. Adding "the", the expression could mean to the last night of a 10-day project, referring to the day 10, regardless of when you are telling the story. 

3. The workers extracted the ore during the night shift.

   - extracted: Monotransitive
   - the ore: DO and NP
   - during the night shift: Adverbial Adjunct of Time and a PP
     - during: head word 
     - the night shift: Object of the prep. and NP
     - the: Central determiner
     - night: Noun Adjunct
     - shift: Head word of the NP

4. This is where I was last time

   - This: Subject and a Demonstrative Pronoun
   - is: CIV
   - where I was last time: Subject Complement, Noun Clause
     - You can expand the sentence "This is **the place in which** I was last time"
     - where: Relative Adverb, and a Locative Adverbial Complement (It provides the mandatory "place" for the verb was)
     - I: Subject
     - was: CIV (It needs a Location)
     - last time: Temporal Adverbial Adjunct and NP
       - last: Post-Determiner
       - time: head word

5. The meeting is tomorrow

   - is: CIV
   - tomorrow: Temporal Adverbial Complement

6. The meeting is at 9 o'clock

   - is: CIV
   - at 9 o'clock: Temporal Adverbial Complement, and a Prepositional Phrase
     - at: head word of the PP
     - 9 o'clock: object of the preposition and NP
       - 9: head of the NP and Cardinal Numeral
       - o'clock: Post-Modifier

7. The workers are drinking water in the office

   - The workers: Subject and NP
   - in the office: Adverbial Adjunct of Place, and a PP

8. The food turned into poison.

   - turned: CIV
   - into poison: **Adverbial Complement** and PP (Adverbial because it represents the result of the transformation)
     - into: head word of the PP
     - poison: object of the preposition

9. He's protecting his house relative to a threat.

   - protecting: Monotransitive
   - his house: DO and NP
   - his: Possessive Central Determiner
   - house: head word of the NP
   - relative to a threat: Adverbial Post-Modifier of the Verb, Adverbial of Relation and PP
     - relative to: Complex Preposition and head of the PP

10. The house relative to the park is mine

    - The house [phrase]: Subject
      - [phrase] = relative to the park: Adjectival Post-Modifier and PP
      - relative to: Complex Preposition and head of the PP
      - the park: Object of the preposition. 
    - is: CIV
    - mine: Subject Complement and Possessive Pronoun
