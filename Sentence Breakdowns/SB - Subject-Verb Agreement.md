1. All the workers extract the ore.

   - All the workers: Subject and NP
   - All: Pre-determiner and Quantifier
   - the: Central determiner
   - workers: Head word of the NP and it is what the verb agrees with
2. All of the workers extract the ore.

   - All of the workers: Subject and NP. A Partitive Structure
   - All: Head of the NP, a Pronoun and a Quantifier
   - of the workers: Adjectival Post-Modifier and a PP. The head of the NP (inside the PP) is what the verb agrees with
3. Two tons of ore were processed.

   - Two tons of ore: Subject and NP, and a Partitive Structure (Measure Partitive)

     - Two: Post-determiner and a Cardinal Numeral
     - tons: head word of the NP and what the verb agrees with

     - of ore: Adjectival Post-Modifier and PP

   - were: Auxiliar Verb in the Passive Voice

   - processed: Past participle Verb in the Passive Voice
4. A number of miners are waiting

   - A number of miners: Subject and NP, and a Quantifying Partitive Structure
     - A number of: Phrasal Quantifier and Central Determiner
     - miners: Head word of the NP 
   - are: Auxiliar verb
   - waiting: Intransitive Verb
5. The number of miners is 50
   - The number of miners: Subject and NP
     - The: Central determiner
     - number: Head word of the NP and what the verb agrees with
     - of miners: Adjectival Post-Modifier and PP
   - is: CIV and Linking Verb
   - 50: Subject Complement and NP (Cardinal Numeral)
