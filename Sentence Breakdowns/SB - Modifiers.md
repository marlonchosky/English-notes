1. The tired workers in the office are drinking water

     - the: Central determiner
     - tired: Adjective
     - workers: head word of the NP, and what the verb agrees with
     - in the office: Adjectival Post-Modifier and PP
       - in: head word of the PP
   - are: Auxiliary verb
   - drinking: Monotransitive Verb
   - water: DO
2. I need a tool to work with
   - need: Monotransitive
   - a tool [phrase]: DO and NP
     - a: Central determiner
     - tool: head word
   - [phrase] : It is an Adjectival Post-Modifier and an Infinitive Phrase
   - to work: head word of the IP, its agent is "I", and it is Intransitive
   - with: Stranded Preposition, and its object is "a tool"

   - at: Stranded Preposition, its object is "a place"
