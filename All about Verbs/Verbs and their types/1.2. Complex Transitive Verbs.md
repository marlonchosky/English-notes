# Blueprint

**Structure**: ***[Subject] + [Verb] + [Direct Object] + [Complement]***

---

# Complement Types

## Locative Complement (<span type="group-1">*DO → Place*</span>)

A mandatory physical position or direction.

- They put the book **on the table**. (A type of *Place Locative Complement*)



## Object Complement

The word or phrase that tells us *the state, identity, or action of the Direct Object*.

### Adjective (Resultative) (<span type="group-1">DO = New State</span>)

A change of state caused by the verb.

- I made him **brave**.



### Adjective (Depictive) (<span type="group-1">DO + New State</span>)

A state maintained or observed.

- I had the room **clean**.



### Noun Phrase (<span type="group-1">DO = Identity</span>)

A new role or identity assigned to the object.

- They elected him **leader**.



###  Infinitive (To or Bare) (<span type="group-1">DO → Action</span>)

An action the object is desired/forced to do.

- I want/made him **to go/go**



### Participle (Present or Past) (<span type="group-1">DO ↔ Action</span>)

A process (active) or a result (passive).

- I saw/had it **moving/done**.
